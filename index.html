JUNE 07, 2023 / Weather data

Weather - Australia 2022

Australia's climate varies greatly throughout the 8 states and territories. The seasons are at opposite times to those in the northern hemisphere. December to February is summer; March to May is autumn; June to August is winter; and September to November is spring.

An exploratory data analysis using python to showcase the weather of australia on various parameters such as Temperature, Dew point, Humidity, Wind Speed.

Dataset aquired from: visualcrossing.com
