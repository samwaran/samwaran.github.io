Projects

Every year there are many death reports of farmers due to pesticide poising as reported by BBC, Times of India, etc. The objective is to provide an autonomous method for precise pesticide use and harvesting the final yield with the use of image processing techniques and thus formed controlled hardware. Images of pest infestation will be taken and pre-programmed commands will be given on the pesticide spraying location. Similarly, harvesting is also done automatically. The data such as “at what part the maximum pest infestation is happening” and “yield of the farm field” can further be plotted over a webpage for the use of researchers and farmers.

Rapid Prototyping is a technology of future according to me. I have developed a cartesian printer with ability to print in 3 dimensions. This printer is developed by me using ground up parts. These crude parts are assembled to provide a simple and precise result out as a print of plastic part. Parameters of the printer: Dimensions: 500x500x300mm (approx.), Voltage: 12V, Current: 5A, Print Material: PLA, ABS

Air pollution has become a common phenomenon everywhere. Specially in the urban areas, air pollution is a real-life problem. A lot of people get sick only due to air pollution. In the urban areas, the increased number of petrol and diesel vehicles and the presence of industrial areas at the outskirts of the major cities are the main causes of air pollution. The problem is seriously intensified in the metropolitan cities. Also, the climate change is now apparent. The governments all around the world are taking every measure in their capacity. India has aimed to do so by 2025. To solve this problem an electronics device is developed based on Arduino, Air Quality Meter. Air Quality meter is meant to be used in house; the device will constantly measure the quality of air inside the house with the help of Air quality sensor (MQ135). The device will show the air quality in PPM on the OLED screen which is mounted on the device.
